STEP ONE: beanine Base

I wanted to keep the hat simple, so for the base beanie
pattern I used shape of fabric's tutorial

On the website they have all these fancy tips on how to sew
the beanie, but since I was using felt for this all I really did was
sew the slit together on each peice and then sew the two peices
together (pics below)



(also I used a whip stitch because i was using a sturdier fabric and it secure
and easy :])


(I have yet to name our hat model. feel free to give suggestions!)

STEP TWO: EARS

The ears are made up of 3 triangles sewn together at each side

(hehe triforce)

NOTE: you want the triangels to be about an 1/8th
the circumference of your head

Repate this process twice and youve got your ears!

STEP THREE : DEATAILS

cut out Garfield's stripes* and ear details.

Sew the ear thingis to the ears and the stripes* to the beanie
and your set!

*(I desided not to add the stripes because Im tired and I think it looks fine
as it is. But feel free to add)

STEP FOUR: ASSEMBLY

Whip stitch the ears on to the beanie
(example of stitch below)

(Tip: make sure to flaten the place you are sewing for easy stitching)
